About Us

Our Story

A School Born from a Community's Dream

Brightpath Academy was established in 2018 with a clear purpose: to create a school that families in the neighbourhood could truly trust — one that combines rigorous academics with genuine warmth, where children are known by name and celebrated as individuals.

From a modest beginning of 60 students, we have grown to serve over 400 young learners from Nursery to Class VIII. Every decision we make is guided by one simple question: what is best for the child?

We are affiliated with the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) and committed to the National Curriculum Framework, delivered through innovative, child-centred teaching methods.

Built for Learning

Spaces That Inspire

Every space — from classrooms to corridors — reflects our commitment to quality education and child well-being.

📚

Well-Stocked Library

Over 5,000 books, periodicals, e-resources and a quiet reading corner for curious minds. Our librarian curates age-appropriate collections for every level and hosts weekly read-aloud sessions.

💻

Computer Laboratory

40-seat air-conditioned computer lab with latest systems and high-speed internet for digital learning, coding classes and research projects from Class III onwards.

🔬

Science Laboratory

Fully equipped physics, chemistry and biology labs enabling hands-on scientific exploration. Children learn by doing — experiments, observations and the joy of discovery.

⚽

Sports Ground

Large multi-sport ground for cricket, football, athletics and annual sports day events. Physical fitness, teamwork and sportsmanship are core to our holistic development philosophy.

Safety First

A Campus You Can Trust

Brightpath Academy is equipped with 24/7 CCTV surveillance across all areas, trained and verified security personnel, RFID-enabled entry gates, and a dedicated first-aid room staffed by a qualified nurse. Your child's safety is our highest priority.

How to Apply

Admission Process

A simple, transparent four-step process to join the Brightpath family.

01

Download Prospectus

Collect or download the admission form and prospectus from the school office or this website.

02

Submit Application

Fill the form completely and submit along with required documents at the admissions office.

03

Interaction Round

Attend the scheduled interaction session for the child and parent with our admissions team.

04

Confirmation

Receive your admission confirmation letter and complete the fee payment to secure the seat.
